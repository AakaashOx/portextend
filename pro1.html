A Mockup website built using ASP.NET and C# with Microsoft SQL Server Management Studio.

Effortless Profile Setup

Create your account in minutes with easy-to-follow steps and secure form validation.

The sign-up process is straightforward, allowing users to create a profile by entering their details and email address, with form validation ensuring strong password requirements are met. After completing the sign-up, users can easily log in using their chosen username and password.

Seamless Flight Booking Experience

Book your flights, view booking history, and cancel tickets with simple steps and secure data entry.

The home page offers options for booking, canceling tickets, and viewing booking history. To book a flight, users select their departure and destination locations, departure time, and number of seats, with a list of available flights provided. After selecting a flight, users can complete the booking by entering the required passenger details, with form validation ensuring accurate data input. Users can also view their booking history and cancel tickets using the ticket ID on the cancellation page.
